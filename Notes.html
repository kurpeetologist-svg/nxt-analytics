The campaign showed that the form step was the bottleneck for a short educational live event: clicks and form opens happened, but submissions did not scale.
19
Future events should start with direct event traffic first, then use Lead Gen Forms for higher-intent follow-up assets such as technical briefs, formulation guides, sample requests, consultations, or gated replay assets.
20
Recommended agile setup: one proven message angle, one high-intent audience, no more than two creative variations; review first 24 hours and scale/cut quickly.
21
