Recommended next moves

Reduce friction first. Capture deeper leads later.

The strongest next campaign is not simply a bigger version of this one. It should apply the funnel learning directly.

01

Reuse the winning message

Start with Ad 1’s hook and creative direction as the baseline rather than reopening the test from scratch.

02

Send short-event traffic direct

Use the LinkedIn Event page or replay page as the first destination and remove unnecessary registration steps.

03

Gate higher-intent assets

Reserve Lead Gen Forms for technical briefs, formulation guides, sample requests, consultations, or gated replay assets.

04

Run a faster test cycle

Use one proven angle, one high-intent audience, and no more than two creative variations. Review within 24 hours.

Strategic takeaway

Campaign Manager leads tell only one part of the story.

Future reporting should separate paid Lead Gen submissions, landing-page clicks, event registrations, replay views, and post-event leads so every stage of the audience journey is visible.
